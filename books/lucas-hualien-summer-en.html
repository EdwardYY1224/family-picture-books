Listen to summer with Lucas

  • Make the sounds: waves go “whoosh,” drops go “drip,” and hands go “pat, pat, pat.”
  • Look and find: Can your child point to the big pebble, little pebble, big wave, and little splash?
  • Talk about feelings: What does warm sunshine, a sea breeze, or cool water feel like?
  • Beach safety: Qixingtan has powerful waves. Watch from a safe distance with a grown-up and stay out of the surf.
  • Water safety: A grown-up stays within arm's reach, even when the water is shallow.
